## Theme of the Night: Selling
Jimmy opened by asking each trainee what they do best — Damian said bumps, Jack said chops, Michael said "jack of all trades, master of none," Hailey said forearms and selling. When Damian bumped, Jimmy noted he should be selling even in training, turning it into an impromptu lesson on the importance of selling. Jimmy and Cody drove home throughout the night that good selling can be the difference between a good and bad match, and that bad selling can make an otherwise good wrestler look really bad.

## Drills Worked
- **Bump + sell**: each trainee bumped and sold it. All did well.
- **Bump-on-contact + sell (lines)**: Jimmy fed each trainee a couple of lines to take and sell. All did well.
- **Spot one** (with instruction to sell everything as if in a real show).
- **Four corner drill** (led by Kytel): a trainee sells their way into all four corners while being worked.
- **Promos**: one-minute individual promos "promoting" this Saturday's show.
