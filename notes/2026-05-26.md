## Coaching Points
- "Shadow bumping" = bumping too early; very frowned upon in the business.
- A snap bump needs to be fast — but that doesn't mean everything else has to speed up too.
- Less float / less distance when taking a shoulder-tackle bump.
- Develop the habit of selling all moves so no-selling never becomes a bad habit.
- Don't get crooked when taking a shoulder-tackle or back-elbow bump.
- A back-elbow bump should be more or less the same as a shoulder-tackle bump.
